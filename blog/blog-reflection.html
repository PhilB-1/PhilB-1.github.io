Reflections on Blog Writing

February 28th, 2016

Writing a blog has been an interesting experience. I didn't particular think I would enjoy it, and I didn't to a certain extent but it taught me a great deal about what I understood. I initially felt like "why am I writing about some things that I could easily find somewhere else on the internet, and probably in more detail." In fact, I used some of their information in creating my blog. What I did learn through this experience is how to really explain something. It also made me reflect on how well I truly knew the information. It often prompted me to do some additional research or review in order to write the blog. The last thing I hope is that people who think like me can relate to my blogs or are able to get some additional understanding.
